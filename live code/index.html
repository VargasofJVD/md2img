Hello, I am Justice Vargas-Drai, and for the past two years,
I have made videos, tutorials and works in graphic designing
and Web development.

I noticed programmers often have difficulty with the design for a
website and/or application. Functionality comes first, and design
is left in the dust.

There is no trick to design, it is just a number of rules and guidelines
to learn and follow, just like any class or object oriented function.
